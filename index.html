FanTouch FAQs

What is a beacon?

Beacons are small devices that use Bluetooth 4.0 technology to send messages (push notification) to any smartphone, without any dropouts. Your beacon range can go up to 100 yards and they operate by proximity and may be fixed or mobile.

Can I move the beacon? 

Yes! You have the option to carry the beacon (gem) with you at all times (recommended) or simply leave it in a high traffic areas to maximize your marketing efforts. 

What if I lose my beacon? 

If you lose your beacon, we will be more than happy to replace it for you. Simply contact us, cover the shipping and handling fee along with the setup fee (total $30) and you will be back to business as usual. 

Do people need an app or need to optin to receive notifications? 

No. Beacon notifications are only viewable by Android phones with Bluetooth and location turned on.

How long do the beacons last? 

Each beacon has a battery life of 24 months or longer.
